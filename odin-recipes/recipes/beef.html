BEEF PILAU RECIPE

This is a mixture of rice and beef (meat)

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of Rice
  • 2 red onions, chopped
  • Pilau masala
  • 3 cloves garlic, grated
  • 1 tablespoon ginger, granted
  • cooking oil
  • 1 tablespoon cloves
  • 2 tablespoon cumin seeds
  • bay leaves
  • tomato paste
  • salt
  • Â¼ cup fresh cilantro roughly chopped
  • 6 cups of meat broth

Preparation

  1. Using a sufuria, heat oil and then add the onions. Fry until the onions start to turn golden brown
  2. Add garlic and ginger, and stir until fragrant.
  3. Add the meat, pilau masala, bay leaves, cilantro, and salt to taste. Cook until the meat browns and caramelises.
  4. Add the blended tomatoes or tomato paste and stir occasionally for about 4 minutes.
  5. Add the meat broth and cook for 8 to 10 minutes bringing to a boil.
  6. Stir in the washed rice, and cover tightly with aluminium foil paper (to keep the steam in) if necessary.
  7. Reduce the heat to low and cook for 20 minutes until the rice is cooked and all the liquid is absorbed.
